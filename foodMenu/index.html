TypeScipt SDK for Dub.co

Whenever you're creating a short link for marketing purposes, it is important to use a custom domain for better brand recognition and click-through rates.

At Dub, we have one of the most generous custom domain limits in the industry – you can add up to 3 custom domains to your workspace for free, and even more with our paid plans. Pro plan: 10 domains Business plan: 40 domains Enterprise plan: Unlimited domains In this guide, we will learn how to add a custom domain to your Dub workspace. If you are using a custom domain on Dub, you can set a custom URL to redirect users to when a short link under your domain is not found.

To set a custom not found URL for your domains, you can follow the steps below: Go to your workspace Domains settings tab. Click on the ⋮ button next to the domain you want to set a custom not found URL for. Select Edit Domain to edit the domain settings. Toggle the Not Found URL section to enable it, and enter the custom URL to redirect users to when a short link under your domain is not found. Click on the Save changes button to save the changes.
